Supabase setup notes

  1. Create a `products` table with columns: name, category, price, rating, badge, description, image_url, video_url, image_hue.
  2. Enable RLS and add a public read policy for active products.
  3. Fill `SUPABASE_URL` and `SUPABASE_ANON_KEY` in app.js to connect.
